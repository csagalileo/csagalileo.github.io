Make one or more of the following enhancements to your PaintPot app:

  • Implement a plus button and implement a minus button, which should add/subtract 1 from the dotsize each time it is clicked.
  • Add a reset button that, when clicked, sets the dotsize back to its initial value (i.e., 5). (HINT: You'll need a second variable here to remember the initial value of the dotsize.)
  • Challenging: A bug is an error or defect, that prevents the app from working the way it is supposed to. In this app, if the user continues to press ButtonMinus, the value of dotsize will eventually become negative. If dotsize is negative, what will be drawn when the user touches the screen? Try fixing this bug by using an If/Else statement. (Hint: The If/Else block is found under Control in the Blocks Editor Toolbox.)
  • Create one or more of your own enhancements for your app.

Perform the following steps to add your completed apps to your Google Sites Portfolio:

  1. In App Inventor, package your app by selecting Build and downloading the .apk to your computer.
  2. Go to Google Drive and upload the .apk file.
  3. Right click on the file in Google Drive. Change the sharing settings on your .apk file.
  4. Right click on the file in Google Drive. Click on Show link. Copy the link.
  5. In Google Sites, on your Creative Projects page, select the Insert menu and choose Link. Paste the link as a Web Addresss. Save the page.
