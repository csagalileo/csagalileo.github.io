This project reinforces the four main phases in the problem-solving process.

  1. Go to the Cornrow Curves website. In your journal, reflect on each of the cultural background sections and explain how the section is connected to cornrow braiding. Share your responses with your group.
  2. Complete the Cornrow Curves design tool tutorial. Explain the following terms in your journal: iteration, dilation, rotation, translation.
  3. In your group, prepare a presentation that includes the following:
    • Cultural background of your assigned section. Your group will be assigned one of the 4 sections to present.
    • Your own designs. Each group member should create at least one design using the math software.
    • Describe each step in the problem-solving process and how it was applied in the creation of your designs.
    • Describe the mathematical concepts used and where and how they are used.
