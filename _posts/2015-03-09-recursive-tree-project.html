This week we’ll be looking at how to solve a computer science problem recursively. The big idea behind recursion is that you can use a block inside of itself! We’re going to practice planning and coding recursive blocks using the example of a tree.

For this project, our goal is to draw a tree using blocks inside of itself. Follow the steps provided in the document to complete your project: Recursive Tree
