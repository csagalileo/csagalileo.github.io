To get started, open App Inventor with the Map Tour Media Only Template in a separate tab and follow along with the following video tutorial. If you prefer, you can click here for a text-based version of the tutorial. 

Create a page named Reflection 14 under the Reflections category of your portfolio and answer the following questions:

  1. What is the list picker component? How was it used in this app?
  2. What is the activity starter component? How was it used in this app?
  3. If you were on a real tour, would the Map Tour app be a good app to have and use? Why or why not?
