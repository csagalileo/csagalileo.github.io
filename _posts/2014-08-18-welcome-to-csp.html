Welcome to Computer Science Principles!

Here are the rules for Classcraft for you to look over: ClasscraftRules

Please complete the following two tasks today:

  • Fill out the Tech Survey. I will use this information to setup your Piazza and Classcraft accounts so do this first.
    • https://docs.google.com/a/galileoweb.org/forms/d/1GfvVp93dEogI4H78bTl9BiwfWla_bLFO8XX6OcAhCQ8/viewform
  • Setup your Google Sites Portfolio. A Google site is a website. We will be using a Google site to share ideas and to present our apps and other work. For each app that you build you will be asked to create a web page that describes your app and gives visitors links to download and run your app. For non-programming lessons you will be asked to answer questions about a reading or to share your reflections about a contemporary issue such as "Privacy".

    By the end of this course you will be very skilled at creating and maintaining your own simple web site. Do your best to make your site attractive and informative. Your site will be one way to express yourself and "Show your stuff!"
    1. Log in to your galileoweb email: mail.galileoweb.org
    2. Click on the apps icon (the 9 little squares in the upper right hand corner). Select the Sites app.
    3. Click Create (on the left hand side).
    4. Enter the name of your site as "[Your Name]'s CSP Portfolio". e.g. Danny Tan's CSP Portfolio
    5. Select a theme for your site (optional but recommended).
    6. Under More Options, enter "computer science" for Site categories and enter "Computer Science Principles Portfolio" for Site description.
    7. Click Create (red button at the top of the page).
    8. Your Google site is now online. Clicking on the blue Share button in the upper right to edit the sharing privileges.
    9. Click on Change... and change the access to "Can view" only. Click Save.
    10. In the "Invite people" box, enter Mr. Tan's email address (tand@galileoweb.org) and click Send to share.
    11. Create the following pages (click the New page button) to be added to your navigation bar
      • Homework
      • Creative Projects
      • Reflections
      • Performance Tasks
    12. On your Home page, add whatever personal information you want to share about yourself, including a photo or image. The photo doesn't have to be a personal photo.
    13. If you would like more details about how to setup or customize your site, here's a Google site for setting up your Google site: https://sites.google.com/site/sitesetupguide/

Again, welcome to CSP! Let's have a fun and exciting year!
