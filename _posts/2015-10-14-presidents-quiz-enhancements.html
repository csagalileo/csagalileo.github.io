Be creative! In this lesson you will add enhancements to the Presidents Quiz app. You are encouraged to discuss your ideas for how to solve these problems with other students.

Objectives: In the lesson you will learn to :

  • solidify your understanding of the quiz app through personalizing it and customizing it;
  • gain experience using the TextToSpeech component;
  • learn to count actions (right/wrong answers) in a complex fashion.
Programming Enhancements
  1. Modify your app so that “correct” and “incorrect” are spoken when the user answers a question.
  2. Modify your app to keep score of how many questions are answered correctly and incorrectly. Be sure and restrict it so that the quiz taker can only receive credit for answering each question once (i.e., if there are three questions, the quiz taker can only be credited with three correct answers). Hint: use a list of true/false (booleans) values to record whether or not each question has already been answered correctly.
  3. Use the President's Quiz as a template to create a quiz app on a topic of your own choosing. Submit your modified app's .apk file.

Hints and suggestions are provided here: Solutions

Complete the reflection questions after you are done with the enhancements. Submit both the .apk file and the reflection on Google Classroom.
